# SOORYA AI — Legal

This repository hosts the public legal documents for the **SOORYA AI** mobile app.

## About SOORYA AI

SOORYA AI is a Vedic astrology app that turns your exact birth chart into deeply
personal readings — full kundli analysis, daily horoscopes, career and
relationship reports, kundli matching, and an AI astrologer you can ask
questions. Every reading is generated from your individual planetary placements,
not generic sun-sign content.

The app is built with React Native (Expo) and is being released on the Google
Play Store.
