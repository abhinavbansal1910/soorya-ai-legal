Account & Data Deletion

How to delete your Soorya AI account

This page explains how to permanently delete your Soorya AI account and all associated data. Soorya AI is developed by Abhinav Bansal.

Option 1 — Delete from within the app

  1. Open the Soorya AI app and sign in.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm when prompted. Your account and data are deleted immediately.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email us from the address linked to your account and we will delete it for you:

Email: sooryaai.in@gmail.com

Please use the subject line Delete my Soorya AI account. We process all email deletion requests within 7 days.

What data is deleted

When you delete your account, the following are permanently and immediately deleted:

  • Your profile (name, email, and login information)
  • Your birth details (date, time, and place of birth)
  • Your birth charts and any saved family member details
  • All generated reports and report history
  • Any wallet balance, referral, and coupon records

What data is kept

No personal data is retained after deletion. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone — there is no additional retention period.

Note: Deleting your account is permanent. If you wish to use Soorya AI again afterwards, you will need to create a new account and re-enter your birth details.
